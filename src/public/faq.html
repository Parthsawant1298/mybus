FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of buses do you offer for hire?

A: We offer a wide range of buses for hire, including mini-buses, standard coaches, luxury coaches, and double-decker buses. Our fleet caters to various group sizes and travel needs.

Q: How do I book a bus for hire?

A: You can book a bus by visiting our website, selecting your preferred bus type, travel dates, and destination. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance with your booking.

Q: What is included in the bus hire price?

A: Our bus hire price typically includes the vehicle rental, driver's services, fuel costs, and basic insurance. Additional services or amenities may be available at an extra cost.

Q: Can I hire a bus for long-distance travel?

A: Yes, we offer bus hire services for both short and long-distance travel. Our buses are well-maintained and equipped for comfortable long-distance journeys.

Q: Are your buses wheelchair accessible?

A: Many of our buses are wheelchair accessible. Please specify your accessibility requirements when making a booking, and we'll ensure you get a suitable vehicle.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?

A: Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking and how far in advance you cancel. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact our customer service for specific details.

Q: Do you provide drivers with all bus hires?

A: Yes, all our bus hires come with professional, experienced drivers who are familiar with the routes and prioritize passenger safety and comfort.

Q: Can I request specific amenities on the bus?

A: Depending on the bus type, we offer various amenities such as Wi-Fi, restrooms, and entertainment systems. You can request specific amenities when making your booking, subject to availability.
